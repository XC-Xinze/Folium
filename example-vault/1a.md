Cards are identified by **Folgezettel** ids: alternating digits and letters.

- `1`, `2`, `3` are top-level cards
- `1a`, `1b` are children of `1`
- `1a1`, `1a2` are children of `1a`

Each card has its own .md file. The system parses ids from filenames.
